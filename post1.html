Why Arcade Means Everything
April 21, 2025
Arcade was never just a book title. It’s a reflection of the chaotic magic that makes life worth living. In a world that constantly pushes us to be linear, I wanted to remind everyone that detours are beautiful — that games, stories, heartbreaks, and victories all live together in one pixelated mess. This post is the first entry in what I hope becomes a long series of reflections. Arcade is a place, a feeling, and maybe even a rebellion.
